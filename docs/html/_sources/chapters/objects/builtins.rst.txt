.. _builtin-types:

Builtin types
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The following builtin types are available

- ``bool``
- ``uint8``
- ``uint16``
- ``uint32``
- ``uint64``
- ``int16``
- ``int32``
- ``int64``
- ``double``
- ``string``

In addition, arrays and dictionaries are supported:

- ``array<type>``
- ``dict<keyType, valueType>`` where ``keyType`` must not be a ``struct``, ``array`` or ``dict``.
